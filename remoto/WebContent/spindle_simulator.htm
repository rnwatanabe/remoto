The Muscle Spindle Simulator


The Muscle Spindle Simulator consists of an implementation in Java of a mathematical model of muscle spindle (Mileusnic et al, 2006) and a user-friendly interface for online physiological simulations of this mechanorreceptor.

This development was made by Vitor M. Chaud and Andre F. Kohn under the support of CNPq and FAPESP (Brazil).

Reference:
Mileusnic et al. Mathematical models of proprioceptors I. Control and transduction in the muscle spindle. Journal of Neurophysiolgy, v. 96, p. 1772-1788, 2006.
